# studio-motion Profile

## Persona

ZugaMotion users are building animated content — motion graphics, animated overlays, and transition sequences. The mental model is closer to a motion designer or content creator than a general consumer: they think in keyframes, easing curves, and composition layers. Sessions involve iterative previewing and adjustment. The UI must support tight feedback loops between parameter editing and live preview, without overwhelming the workspace.

## Accent rationale

ZugaMotion is the third creative family sibling alongside ZugaImage and ZugaVideo. It uses the same violet ramp to maintain creative family coherence. Of the three, ZugaMotion's niche is the most technically distinct — animation parameters, timeline scrubbing — but the color convention says "you are in the creative toolset" regardless of the specific output format. See `acc08` in §5.
