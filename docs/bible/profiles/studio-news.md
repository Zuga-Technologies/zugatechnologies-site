# studio-news Profile

## Persona

ZugaNews users are consuming curated news and intelligence — headlines, summaries, and topic feeds filtered by their interests or work context. The mental state is passive and scanning: they open the studio to catch up, not to act. The UI should feel like an editorial reading surface — clean, typographically focused, with minimal chrome. A strong brand accent would compete with the content; the surface should disappear behind the words.

## Accent rationale

ZugaNews inherits master cyan — it earns no override. Editorial neutrality is the design constraint: any color with strong category associations (red=alert, green=markets, violet=creative) would color the reader's perception of the content. Master cyan is the most neutral Zuga hue and avoids any partisan or domain-specific signal. See `acc16` in §5.

## Deviations from §15 whitelist

No deviations. This profile overrides only `--accent-*` — and in this case, not even that; it consumes Tier 2 defaults as-is.

## Density posture

Comfortable density. No override.
