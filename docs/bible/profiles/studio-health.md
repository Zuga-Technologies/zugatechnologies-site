# studio-health Profile

## Persona

ZugaHealth users are biometric-aware individuals tracking body data — BMI trends, Fitbit step counts, caloric intake, sleep quality scores. The context is more analytical than ZugaLife's habit-streak loop: they arrive to read numbers and assess progress over time. The mental state can be either clinical (reviewing a week of data after a workout) or anxious (checking weight after a bad week). The UI should feel trustworthy and precise, not gamified.

## Accent rationale

ZugaHealth shares the wellness family with ZugaLife and uses the same mint ramp. The shared accent communicates family cohesion — both studios live under the same wellness umbrella — but the two have distinct product personas (biometrics vs. habit loops). Mint signals health and vitality; the differentiation from ZugaLife comes through layout and content density, not color. See `acc04` in §5.

## Deviations from §15 whitelist

No deviations. This profile overrides only `--accent-*` (mint ramp).

## Density posture

Comfortable density. No override.
