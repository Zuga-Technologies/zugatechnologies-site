# studio-image Profile

## Persona

ZugaImage users are generating AI imagery — creating assets for social, experimenting with prompts, browsing a gallery of previously generated work. The context is exploratory and creative: they're playing with ideas, not shipping code or tracking biometrics. The UI should feel like a creative workbench — dark enough that images pop, accented with a color that signals imagination rather than productivity.

## Accent rationale

ZugaImage is in the creative family and uses violet. Violet historically signals artistic creativity and imaginative thinking, distinct from the blues of dev-productivity tools and the greens of wellness. It groups ZugaImage with its creative siblings — ZugaVideo and ZugaMotion — into a recognizable sub-brand cluster. All three creative studios share the same violet ramp; differentiation happens through layout and iconography. See `acc06` in §5.

## Deviations from §15 whitelist

No deviations. This profile overrides only `--accent-*` (violet ramp).

## Density posture

Comfortable density. No override.
